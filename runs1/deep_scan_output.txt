═══ INTERPRETATION GUIDE ═══

Repeating structure with stride → array of identical units (SMs, LTC slices, etc.)
Falcon pattern (+0x030/+0x100) → microcontroller-based engine
Dense live registers → active complex engine (not just config)
R/W registers → control/config (vs R/O status/caps)
High bit count in cap regs → many features enabled

Key question: is 0x300000-0x3F0000 a known engine (XBAR/HSHUB/PRI)
or something undocumented (DMA accelerator / TMA / other)?
